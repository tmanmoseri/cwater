Vision

C-Water Construction executives see the company being the major provider of quality and affordable buildings to clients across South Africa and the neighbouring countries in the foreseeable future. This will be accompanied by the great customer service and client satisfaction that the company has established its name upon.

Mission

Our mission is provide quality and affordable building services to our clients with ease. To simplify the process of getting a new house, a warehouse or any building by packaging everything required in the construction of a building into one. Our mission is to grow the business to ensure many clients enjoy the benefits of our hard work.

Services

Our products range from the initial stage of construction which starts with plan and design, to the very end where the complete building is constructed.
This includes:

  • Plan, design and printing of blueprint
  • Application for approval of the building blueprint
  • Quantity survey and estimation of building cost
  • Material orders and purchase (client has option of purchasing through us or by themselves.)
  • Geotechnical survey and land acquisition
  • Complete construction of building, roofing and painting
  • Construction of wall fencing
  • Landscaping of the yard (done by external contractor)
The geotechnical survey is done by external contractor. We do not assist in financing or loan applications.

The founder of C-water hand delivered all the Plans he designed to ensure customers can approve or have the chance to change anything that my not be part of their plan. This is the kind of client service our founder expects from every employee.
